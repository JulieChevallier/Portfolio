Hi! I'm Julie Chevallier, a 21 year old student. I'm currently in my second year of Computer Science at the IMT Mines Alès. I develop things on my free time. I like to mix science and mathematics with programming and I'm always looking for new challenges. Passionate about new technologies, my goal is to continue my studies by pursuing a PhD, allowing me to contribute to cutting-edge research while expanding my expertise in computer science and networks.

Motivated and ambitious, I am constantly looking for new opportunities to enrich my knowledge and develop my skills. Convinced that IT is a constantly evolving field, I have the ambition to be part of those who contribute to its progress.

I aspire to work in research, explore new technologies and find innovative solutions to current problems. As a woman, I aspire to play an active role in the advancement of computer science and to participate in the creation of a stimulating environment focused on research and innovation.

I regularly participate in Hackathon and Game Jam events in order to enrich myself personally but also with the aim of meeting competent and passionate people! I recently participated in the CodeGameJam which I won with my team thanks to my project Time Bomb.
